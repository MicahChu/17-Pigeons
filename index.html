17 Pigeons


17 Pigeons is a 16 year old electronic music composer and producer from Boston. Starting with no previous musical experience, he quickly picked up songwriting and since then has been constantly making track after track. 5 months after he began, 17 Pigeons released his remix of Stollaz's Clockwork Monsters. Following this release he has many more coming that are sure to impress.

Logo and album arts designed and drawn by Ryan D'Souza.

Music


17 Pigeons mainly produces house of all types, but has occasionally made moombah, dubstep, and even trap. His music can be found on his SoundCloud as well as on Spotify and other services.
